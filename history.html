Our History

Work Cat Trans Gulf (WCTG) is a short sea shipping operation that provides trans-Gulf of Mexico container and roll on/roll off freight transportation services between the ports of Tampa, Florida and Brownsville, Texas utilizing its own game-changing proprietary vessel design known as the “Work Cat.” The Company is the exclusive owner and operator of the fast and highly efficient Work Cat cargo vessel—a modern 400 foot catamaran capable of transporting 300 truckload equivalent shipments. Through its relationships with third party logistics providers, manufactured product shippers, and intermodal carriers, WCTG offers a completely unique alternative form of transportation capable of delivering fast, highly reliable service at low cost relative to all other land and sea-based transport modes. The economies of scale achieved by the Company’s unique catamaran cargo vessel enables the generation of a growing revenue stream and well above average return on investment.

These key transport market influences include

1. Increased Gulf of Mexico container traffic due to Panama Canal expansion (2016)

2. High and rising transportation energy prices

Increasing transportation-related air pollution

4. High and worsening congestion along coastal interstate highways and railroad hubs

5. Deteriorating highway and railroad infrastructure

6. Federal regulation and legislation that encourages short sea shipping

7. Little or no direct competition 8. National imperative for safer highways, i.e., fewer trucks.

WCTG's catamaran vessel

WCTG's catamaran vessel operation represents by far the most economically viable mode of cargo transportation consistent with these market factors. To date the Company has conducted extensive due diligence related to customer and market development, labor and equipment costing, selection of port facilities, and, above all, new vessel selection. Revenue is generated through the diversion of cargo from traditional modes to the Company’s lower cost Work Cat vessel operation—a win-win for both WCTG and its customer partners.
